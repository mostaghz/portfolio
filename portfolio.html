Abq Tattoo(h)ers

Conceptualized and built during the first half of Deep Dive Coding’s Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, Abq Tattoo(h)ers is a growing directory highlighting Albuquerque’s female tattoo artists. Tattooing, like software development and tech, is a male-dominated industry. As a woman, tattoo enthusiast, and developer, it’s important I work with and promote as many female artists as I can. This is a much needed resource that I wish I had, so I’m thrilled to put this out into the world for everyone’s benefit!

COMING SOON: I’m rebranding the site as NM Tattoo(h)ers to include artists in Santa Fe, Taos, Las Cruces, and more! This change will also include a new multi-page design and new search/filter features.

Built with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap

Plate it Forward

Plate it Forward is a web application and program that tackles hunger and food waste in Albuquerque by partnering with community centers and local restaurants to provide meals to those in need. Conceptualized and built with Erin Ruiz and Sergio Salas during Deep Dive Coding’s Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, we honed our developer skills and coded the entire stack in seven weeks.

AWARDS: 2nd place at 2022-2023 UNM/CNM's Mobile App Contest

Built with JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Redux, PostgreSQL, and Bootstrap

Bob's Bipsums

Bob’s Bipsums is a randomized lorem ipsum generator using quotes from every episode (yes, all 13 seasons!) of Bob’s Burgers. I’m obsessed with the show – I even have a tattoo of Bob and Louise cosplaying as Hawk and Chick. I’ll continue to add more quotes for every upcoming season. Cheeky ipsum is the best, and I can’t wait to make the site live!
