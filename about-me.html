About Me

What’s up, everyone? Thanks for stopping by!

My name is Alex Mostaghni (pronounced “moe-stag-knee” -- it's Persian). I’m a nonprofit event coordinator and donor relations expert turned full stack web and game developer. The first code I ever wrote was HTML with inline styling for my MySpace page. I didn’t start coding again until enrolling in Deep Dive Coding’s Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp in October 2022. Fast forward to today, I’ve built a handful of web applications and have one video game in development. I’m also an active member of the Rubber Ducks, Google Developer Group, and Albuquerque Software Social Club, as well as an alumni mentor for the bootcamp.

When I’m not coding or partaking in developer-related shenanigans, I:

  • rewatch Bob’s Burgers for the nth time.
  • order pineapple on my pizza.
  • go on outdoor adventures with my two rescue pug-mixes.
  • butcher beloved songs during karaoke.
  • brainstorm and research tattoo ideas/artists for my next appointment.

I’m always down to learn a new programming language and collaborate on a project. Reach out so we can work together!
